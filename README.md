# Coffee-Sales-Analysis
This project transforms raw transaction data into actionable business insights through systematic exploratory data analysis. By analyzing 1,133 coffee sales transactions spanning March to July 2024, the analysis uncovers purchasing patterns, peak demand periods, product preferences, and payment trends to optimize inventory management, staffing allocation, and revenue strategies.

# Key Insights:-

-Total Transactions -  1133 

-Analysis Period -  5 months (Mar-Jul 2024) -	Captures quarterly seasonal patterns

-Card Payment Rate 	-  92.1%(1,044 transactions)	 - Strong digital payment adoption

-Top Product	-  Americano with Milk	- 23.65% market share

-Peak Hour	-  10:00 AM	- 133 transactions (11.7% of daily volume)

-Second Peak	-  7:00 PM	- 96 transactions (8.5% of daily volume)

-Busiest Day	-  Tuesday	- 185 transactions

-Product Range	-  8 varieties	- Diverse menu strategy

-Heavy reliance on digital payment infrastructure; robust system redundancy essential
